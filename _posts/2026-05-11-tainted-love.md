This week's instalment of Music Monday is *[Tainted Love](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tainted_Love)* by
[Gloria Jones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gloria_Jones) from their 1964 album,
[My Bad Boy's Comin' Home](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tainted_Love). The [Soft Cell](https://www.softcell.co.uk/)
version is from their 1981 album, [Non-Stop Erotic Cabaret](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Non-Stop_Erotic_Cabaret).

Jones recorded this in 1964 as the B-side to "My Bad Boy's Comin' Home" — it failed to chart and was largely forgotten. It found a second life as a Northern soul dancefloor staple in northern England, which is how Soft Cell came to discover it. Their synth-pop cover reached number one in 17 countries, spent a then-record 43 consecutive weeks on the US Billboard Hot 100, and became the best-selling UK single of 1981. Jones herself later joined Soft Cell's Marc Almond on stage to perform it.
