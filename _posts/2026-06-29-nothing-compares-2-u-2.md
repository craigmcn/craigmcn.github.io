This week's instalment of Music Monday is [*Nothing Compares 2 U*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nothing_Compares_2_U) by [The Family](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Family_(band)) from their 1985 album, [The Family](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Family_(The_Family_album)). The [Sinéad O'Connor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sin%C3%A9ad_O%27Connor) version is from their 1990 album, [I Do Not Want What I Haven't Got](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/I_Do_Not_Want_What_I_Haven%27t_Got).

Prince wrote the song in 1984 and gave it to his side project The Family, who recorded it for their self-titled debut album but never released it as a single. It languished in relative obscurity until Sinéad O'Connor recorded her version five years later. O'Connor's recording topped the charts in over a dozen countries and won Video of the Year at the 1990 MTV VMAs, making it one of the most successful covers in pop history.
